About the Author

Bonavie Miles Mata Perez is a first-year Civil Engineering student at Western Mindanao State University with a heart for social equity. She is a dedicated advocate for Affordable and Safe Housing, believing that structural integrity is a fundamental human right. By merging her technical studies with humanitarian goals, she aims to design resilient and low-cost shelters for underserved communities. She aspires to use her engineering career to build a future where every family has a secure and dignified place to call home.

She firmly believes that a house is not just a structure, but the essential foundation for human dignity and health. She maintains that engineering excellence should be measured by its ability to serve the most vulnerable members of society. Above all, she is convinced that through innovative design and political will, safe shelter can be made accessible to everyone regardless of their income.
