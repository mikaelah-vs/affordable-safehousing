References

Affordable and Safe Housing

Global Housing Crisis & Human Rights

UN-Habitat Strategic Plan (2026–2029): This plan specifically addresses the global housing crisis, which currently affects nearly 2.8 billion people worldwide. It reinforces that housing is the foundation for health, dignity, and opportunity.

UN Human Rights Office (OHCHR): Provides the legal framework for "The Right to Adequate Housing," which includes legal security of tenure and habitability.

Urban Policy & Affordability Models

Grounded Solutions Network: A leading resource for Community Land Trusts (CLTs) and Inclusionary Housing policies. They provide research on how these models preserve permanent affordability.

National League of Cities (NLC): Their 2026 briefs discuss the role of local governments in repurposing public land and managing inclusionary zoning to bridge the "rent-trap" gap.

Resilience and Sustainable Infrastructure

UNDP (United Nations Development Programme): Recent 2026 reports highlight programs for "Housing Recovery and Essential Household Items" that integrate climate-resilient designs for flood-prone areas.

Social and Faith-Based Advocacy

Catholic Bishops’ Conference of the Philippines (CBCP): Their pastoral statements frequently address the "right to a home" and the dignity of informal settlers.
